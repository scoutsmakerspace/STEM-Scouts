## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Learn how to care for an animal.
- 2. 2. Help to take care of that animal for four weeks.
- 3. 3. Keep a note of how you have looked after the animal for four weeks.
- 4. 4. Tell others in your Colony or Lodge about the animal.
