## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Design a cover for your favourite book.
- 2. 2. Read at least six books.
- 3. 3. Show that you know how to look after a book.
- 4. 4. Make a bookmark and explain what bookmarks are for.
