## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Help to put up a tent.
- 2. 2. Collect wood and help to build a fire.
- 3. 3. Cook on a fire or barbeque.
- 4. 4. Learn how to tie a reef knot.
- 5. 5. Take part in a small pioneering project.
- 6. 6. Start your own camp blanket.
- 7. 7. Visit a Cub, Scout or Group camp.
