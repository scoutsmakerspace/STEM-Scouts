## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Learn how to make a phone call.
- **2.** 2. Learn your phone number.
- **3.** 3. Learn how to send an email or text message.
- **4.** 4. Spell your name in Morse code, semaphore, the phonetic alphabet, or using pictures and symbols.
- **5.** 5. Try to work out a simple message given to you in Morse code, semaphore, the phonetic alphabet, or using pictures and symbols.
