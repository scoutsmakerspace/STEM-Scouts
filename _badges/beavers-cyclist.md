## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Find out why it’s important to wear safety equipment like a helmet, reflective clothing and lights.
- 2. 2. Show how to put on and wear a cycle helmet correctly.
- 3. 3. Show how to pump up a tyre.
- 4. 4. Go for a short ride in a safe place.
