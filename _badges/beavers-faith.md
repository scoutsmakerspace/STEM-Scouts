## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Write a prayer or reflection.
- 2. Read or share a prayer or reflection.
- 3. Find and tell a story about doing your best and helping other people.
- 4. Visit a place of worship and tell other Beaver Scouts about your visit.
