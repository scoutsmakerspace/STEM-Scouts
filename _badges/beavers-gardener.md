## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Find out what happens to trees in each of the four seasons.
- 2. 2. Learn what tools you need to garden and how to use some of the tools safely.
- 3. 3. Find out what plants need to grow.
- 4. 4. Help to look after a garden or allotment for two months.
- 5. 5. Keep a record of what you have done and the changes you see.
