## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Take part in three agility activities.
- **2.** 2. Learn about a variety of healthy foods and make a snack for yourself using some of these foods.
- **3.** 3. Monitor heartbeat before and after completing a range of different physical activities and understand the reasons for change.
- **4.** 4. Promote healthy eating and exercise to others.
