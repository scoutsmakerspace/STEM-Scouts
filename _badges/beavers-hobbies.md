## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Choose a hobby to take part in for at least six weeks.
- 2. 2. Show that you have stuck with your hobby.
- 3. 3. Show and tell the Colony, your Lodge or leaders about your chosen hobby.
