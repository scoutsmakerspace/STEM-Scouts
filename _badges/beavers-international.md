## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Learn a song, play a game or perform a dance from another country.
- **2.** 2. Find out about Beaver Scouts, or younger Scouts if they don’t have Beaver Scouts, in another country.
- **3.** 3. Play a game to match flags and their countries.
- **4.** 4. Try different kinds of food from another country.
- **5.** 5. Learn how to say hello and goodbye in a language other than your own.
