## Requirements

- **1.** Tell others what the Green Cross Code is about.
- **2.** Tell others what the Water Safety Code is about.
- **3.** Say what you should do if a stranger walks over to you and tries to talk to you.
- **4.** Point out dangers around the home or at your meeting place.
- **5.** Tell others what you should do if there is a fire.
- **6.** Find out how to call the emergency services.
- **7.** Tell others what you should do at a roadside and at a railway level crossing.
