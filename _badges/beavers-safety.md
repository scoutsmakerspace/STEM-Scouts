- **1.** 1. Tell others what the Green Cross Code is about.

- **5.** 5. Tell others what you should do if there is a fire.
- **6.** 6. Find out how to call the emergency services.
- **7.** 7. Tell others what you should do at a roadside and at a railway level crossing.
