- **1.** 1. Find out what Earth looks like from space; the shapes of the continents, oceans, forests and cities.

- **3.** 3. Find out what it’s like living on the International Space Station.
- **4.** 4. Explore how different types of rockets have been used over the years to get people and objects into space.
- **5.** 5. Explore the planets in our solar system.
