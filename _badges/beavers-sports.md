## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Play a team sport with the Beavers in your Colony.
- 2. 2. Understand the rules of that sport.
- 3. 3. Take part in a sport or physical activity regularly for at least six weeks.
- 4. 4. Take part in a match, competition, show or pass an exam or achieve a grading for that sport.
- 5. 5. Show your Lodge, Colony or Explorer Scout Young Leader the skills you have learnt and the equipment you need for your sport.
