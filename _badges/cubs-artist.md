## Requirements

- **1.** Visit an art gallery
- **2.** Design and build a model
- **3.** Draw or paint a picture of still life or a landscape
- **4.** Imagine an event, character or scene. Now draw it using a pencil, brush, pen or crayon
- **5.** Design and make a greetings card
- **6.** Make a model out of clay
- **7.** Make a display of photographs on a subject that interests you
- **8.** Make a poster advertising Scouting or a Cub Scout event
- **9.** Design and make a decorated book cover
- **10.** Make a video on a subject that interests you
