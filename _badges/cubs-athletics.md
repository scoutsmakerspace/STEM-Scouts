## Requirements

- **1.** Take part in a proper warm-up and cool down routine, using all the main muscle groups.
- **2.** Explain the best clothes to wear for athletics and how to be safe.
- **3.** Take part in a throwing activity
- **4.** Take part in two running activities
- **5.** Take part in a team activity
- **6.** Do a jumping activity and go as far or as high as you can go
