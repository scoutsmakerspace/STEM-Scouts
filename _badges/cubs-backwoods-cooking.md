## Requirements

- **1.** Show how to light a fire
- **2.** Help someone prepare a fire for cooking on
- **3.** Cook something using a billy can
- **4.** Cook something in the embers of a fire
- **5.** Cook something on a stick
- **6.** Show how to make the fire safe when you have finished with it. Extinguish the fire and make the area safe
