## Requirements

- **1.** Get someone to give you directions or instructions to do something.
- **2.** Get someone you know to give you a call.
- **3.** Show you can send emails and text messages.
- **4.** Introduce yourself to an adult who helps out with your Pack
- **5.** Write three simple messages using codes, ciphers, invisible ink or semaphore
- **6.** Report on a local event, either past or present.
- **7.** Find out how people with a visual or hearing impairment communicate
- **8.** Hold a simple conversation in another language
- **9.** Pass a message to someone using amateur radio
- **10.** Take part in Jamboree On The Air (JOTA) or Jamboree On The Internet (JOTI)
- **11.** Make contact with another Pack
- **12.** Tell a story about an experience you’ve shared with your leaders  and other Cubs
- **13.** Get an adult or Young Leader to give you a message
