## Requirements

- **1.** Get someone to give you directions or instructions to do something.
- **2.** Get someone you know to give you a call.
- **3.** Show you can send emails and text messages.
- **4.** Introduce yourself to an adult who helps out with your Pack
- **5.** Pass a message to someone using amateur radio
- **6.** Take part in Jamboree On The Air (JOTA) or Jamboree On The Internet (JOTI)
- **7.** Make contact with another Pack
- **8.** Tell a story about an experience you’ve shared with your leaders  and other Cubs
- **9.** Get an adult or Young Leader to give you a message
