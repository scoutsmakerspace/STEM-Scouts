## Requirements

- **1.** Find out about two different disabilities that you could learn more about
- **2.** Find out more about a piece of equipment, assistive technology or adaptation for disabled people that can be used at school or work.
- **3.** Find out about the best ways to communicate with different disabled people.
- **4.** Choose your favourite activity, hobby or sport and think of ways it could be made more accessible
