## Requirements

- **1.** Help to plan and perform some recorded entertainment, like a video or audio story.
- **2.** Take part in a show, concert or band performance
- **3.** Make up and perform a dance to a piece of music of your choice
- **4.** Help plan and perform a series of magic tricks.
- **5.** Help to make up a mime or play, then perform it
- **6.** Perform a puppet play or shadowgraph using puppets that you have made.
- **7.** Sing two songs
- **8.** Perform some folk or traditional dance
- **9.** Make a selection of simple rhythm instruments
