- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Learn how to separate recyclable and non-recyclable rubbish ready for collection
- 2. Find out how to reduce the energy and water you use in your home.
- 3. Find out about one type of renewable energy.
- 4. Take part in two projects with a group of other people
