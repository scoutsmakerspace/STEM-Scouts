## Requirements

- **1.** Visit a fire station or invite a firefighter to your meeting place or Llarn about some of the other things that firefighters do
- **2.** Take part in a fire drill at your meeting place.
- **3.** Make an emergency escape plan for your home.
- **4.** Find out why it’s important to use smoke and heat detectors
- **5.** Point out three possible dangers at home or your meeting place that could start a fire.
- **6.** Find out how to keep a campfire safe for everyone.
- **7.** Explain how you should behave around a bonfire and fireworks.
