## Requirements

- **1.** Find out what seasons are best to grow a selection of six fruits and vegetables.
- **2.** Learn what tools you need to garden (a minimum of three) and show that you know how to use them safely.
- **3.** Help to look after and grow at least one item in a garden or allotment for two months
- **4.** Design plant labels for the plants you are looking after or growing
- **5.** Understand what mini beasts are and why they are good for our gardens and outdoor spaces.
