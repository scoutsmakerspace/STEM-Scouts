## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Plan, cook and serve a simple one-course meal.
- 2. 2. Wash up afterwards.
- 3. 3. Help sort out the washing. Load and unload the washing machine.
- 4. 4. Iron at least two items.
- 5. 5. Sew on a button.
- 6. 6. Help to clean and tidy a living room.
- 7. 7. Clean at least two items in your home.
- 8. 8. Take sheets, pillow covers and the duvet cover off a bed and help to put clean ones on. Make your own bed for a week.
