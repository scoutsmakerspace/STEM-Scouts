## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Find out what to do about a burst water pipe, gas leak or electricity power failure in your home.
- 2. 2. Identify the common causes of accidents in the home.
- 3. 3. Find out how to protect your home from crime.
- 4. 4. Find out where the nearest public telephone box is to your house.
- 5. 5. Make a list of useful emergency numbers.
