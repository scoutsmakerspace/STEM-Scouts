## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Draw or create the World Scout Badge.
- 2. 2. Make, build or draw something to represent a country.
- 3. 3. Explore another country’s traditions and culture around food and eating.
- 4. 4. Take part in the activities of a celebration or festival that usually takes place in another country.
