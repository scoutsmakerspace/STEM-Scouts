## Requirements

- **1.** Learn how to identify six different living things
- **2.** Over three months, visit the same natural area at least four times.
- **3.** Learn the countryside code and how to follow it.
- **4.** Make a hedgehog, ladybird or bee house.
- **5.** Create a piece of art using natural material. You could do a bark or leaf rubbing, dried flower picture or maybe a collage.
- **6.** With an adult pick wild edible berries, leaves or fruit. Use them in a recipe.
- **7.** Use a dye that uses natural ingredients to colour a piece of material.
- **8.** Make a feeding station for birds. Get permission to hang it in a good position.
