## Requirements

- **1.** Take an active part in at least three nights away, on camps or Pack holidays.
- **2.** Cook a meal with your Six
- **3.** Learn and follow the Countryside Code
- **4.** Help to pitch and strike your tent
- **5.** Show that you know how to look after yourself and be safe at camp
- **6.** Show that you know how to keep your tent and kit safe, tidy and secure,
- **7.** Show you know what things you need to do to, look after your campsite, and that you can put them into practise,
- **8.** Build a shelter big enough for two Cubs
- **9.** Using bamboo canes, rubber bands or simple lashings complete a simple pioneering project or make a camp gadget,
- **10.** Show that you know how to treat mild burns, scalds, cuts or grazes and make a call to the emergency services
- **11.** Take part in a wide game
- **12.** Cook a backwoods meal
- **13.** Using knots that you've learned, make a simple camp gadget, like a flagpole
- **14.** Take part in a campfire sing-along or other entertainment
- **15.** Care for your personal equipment while at camp
- **16.** Build a bivouac and sleep in it
