## Requirements

- **1.** Show that you know how to use and look after a digital camera or smartphone camera.
- **2.** 2. Take at least five photos of your local area that could be used on a tourist leaflet or website.
- **3.** 3. Take photos while you’re on a trip, camp or another event.
- **1.** Create two photos of the same thing, one in colour and one in black and white. Compare how the effects change the way the final picture looks.
- **2.** Edit a photo using a smartphone or editing software. Explain what you have changed and why.
- **3.** Make a short film on a subject of your choice.
- **4.** Using a series of photos, make a short animation sequence.
