## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 2. Tell your leader about the sports you take part in.
- 2. 3. Show that you’re fairly skilled in your sports.
- 3. 4. Bring the clothing and equipment for the sport you’ve chosen along to a meeting.
- 4. Find out as much as you can about your sports, especially the rules.
- 5. 5. Tell a leader about the training and preparation you take part in for your chosen sport.
