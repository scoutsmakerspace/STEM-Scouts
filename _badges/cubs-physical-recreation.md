## Requirements

- **1.** Tell your leader about the sports you take part in.
- **2.** Show that you’re fairly skilled in your sports.
- **3.** Bring the clothing and equipment for the sport you’ve chosen along to a meeting.
- **4.** Find out as much as you can about your sports, especially the rules.
- **5.** Tell a leader about the training and preparation you take part in for your chosen sport.
