## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Draw or take photos of 10 different traffic signs.
- 2. 2. Show how to use the Green Cross Code.
- 3. 3. Tell a leader why it’s important to have different types of pedestrian crossing.
- 4. 4. Show that you know how to behave safely as a car passenger.
