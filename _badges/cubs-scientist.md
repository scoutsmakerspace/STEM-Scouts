## Requirements

- **1.** Find out what happens when you add salt to water.
- **2.** Show how vinegar reacts with different items and explain what happens. The different items could be steel wool, sodium bicarbonate or old dirty coins.
- **3.** Compare the density of water to the density of other liquids. Show how these liquids react to each other.
- **4.** Make a pH indicator solution. Use it to test the acidity or alkalinity of other liquids.
- **5.** Grow crystals.
- **6.** Another activity agreed with your leader
