## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Use a skateboard, scooter, or in-line, quad or ice skates, on at least three occasions.
- 2. 2. Learn some safety rules about skating on the road and in other public places.
- 3. 3. Show how to start, stop and turn safely.
- 4. 4. Show how to fall safely and regain balance.
- 5. 5. Show how you do three different manoeuvres.
