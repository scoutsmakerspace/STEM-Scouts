## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Follow your chosen sport for at least three months.
- 2. 2. Find out the rules of the sport. Explain them to an adult.
- 3. 3. Show you have a good knowledge of the teams and/or personalities in your chosen sport.
- 4. 4. Explain what equipment is needed for the sport.
- 5. 5. List some major events for the sport of your choice.
- 6. 6. Describe events that you have attended for your chosen sport.
