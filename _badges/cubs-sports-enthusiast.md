## Requirements

- **1.** Follow your chosen sport for at least three months.
- **2.** Find out the rules of the sport. Explain them to an adult.
- **3.** Show you have a good knowledge of the teams and/or personalities in your chosen sport.
- **4.** Explain what equipment is needed for the sport.
- **5.** List some major events for the sport of your choice.
- **6.** Describe events that you have attended for your chosen sport.
