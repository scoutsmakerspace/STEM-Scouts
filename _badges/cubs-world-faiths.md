## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Visit a place of worship which is important to one of the World religions.
- 2. 2. Meet someone who belongs to one of the World religions, or has a set of beliefs which are different to your own.
- 3. 3. Find out about the places of religious significance for a World religion.
- 4. 4. Find out about the religious festivals and customs linked to a World religion.
