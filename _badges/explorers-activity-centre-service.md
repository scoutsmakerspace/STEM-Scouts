## Requirements

- MANDATORY (all required):
- 1. Work for at least five days (on at least two different occasions) at a  Group, District, County, Area or National Scout Activity Centre or campsite, helping the warden or manager however they need you.
- 2. Explain the maintenance needed for essential campsite equipment PLUS 1 of the following:
- 1. Make a booking for someone wanting to use the site
- 2. Show how to restore a camping area after use, including sowing grass
- 3. Show how you prevent pipes freezing during the winter and what steps to take when pipes burst
- 4. The importance of having clean toilets
- 5. Chemicals – what to use and how to use them safely
- 6. How to unblock a drain and prevent further blockages
- 7. The need for good site drainage and keeping ditches clear
- 8. How to dispose of refuse and use an effective recycling system
