## Requirements

- **1.** Work for at least five days (on at least two different occasions) at a  Group, District, County, Area or National Scout Activity Centre or campsite, helping the warden or manager however they need you.
- **2.** Explain the maintenance needed for essential campsite equipment
- **3.** Make a booking for someone wanting to use the site
- **4.** Show how to restore a camping area after use, including sowing grass
- **5.** Show how you prevent pipes freezing during the winter and what steps to take when pipes burst
- **6.** The importance of having clean toilets
- **7.** Chemicals – what to use and how to use them safely
- **8.** How to unblock a drain and prevent further blockages
- **9.** The need for good site drainage and keeping ditches clear
- **10.** How to dispose of refuse and use an effective recycling system
