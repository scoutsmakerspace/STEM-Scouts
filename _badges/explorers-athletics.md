## Requirements

- **1.** Run through an appropriate warm-up routine that involves gentle stretching and loosening exercises, using all the main muscle groups.
- **2.** Discuss the safety rules for athletics, particularly the throwing and jumping events.
- **3.** Demonstrate an athletic discipline to other Explorers and encourage them to try it out.
- **4.** Take part in at least two competitions or demonstrations.
