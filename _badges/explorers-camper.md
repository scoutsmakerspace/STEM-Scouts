## Requirements

- **1.** Camp under canvas for a total of 20 nights or more.
- **1.** Are, simple repair and storage of your tent and cordage, including whipping, splicing, hanking, coiling and safety inspections.
- **2.** Safe storage and handling of fuels such as methylated spirits, paraffin, petrol and gas.
- **3.** Care of cooking stoves and utensils, including simple repairs, cleaning and general maintenance.
- **4.** Storage of a section’s training and games equipment.
- **1.** discuss all aspects of the camp with an adult leader
- **2.** manage the budget for the camp
- **3.** choose the campsite, carrying out a risk assessment of the camp and activities
- **4.** make sure all necessary equipment is organised
- **5.** plan a menu and organise the shopping.
- **6.** organise the pitching of the campsite
- **7.** make sure that camp hygiene standards are met
- **8.** make sure that all equipment is stored correctly
- **9.** build a pioneering project, for example a flagpole or a gateway
- **10.** demonstrate safe knife and axe use
- **11.** plan a cooking and cleaning rota and cook at least one of the meals yourself
- **12.** organise the striking of camp and make sure the site is left as you would wish to find it.
- **13.** make sure that all equipment is stored correctly
- **14.** evaluate how the camp went by discussing it with the other participants.
