## Requirements

- **1.** Make at least four trips underground, with a properly authorised party, to two different cave systems and kept a log. If you hold the Scout Caver Activity Badge you don’t need to complete these four trips.
- **2.** Explain how caves are formed.
- **3.** Rig and use ladder pitch under supervision.
- **4.** Take two additional caving trips as part of a properly authorised party.
- **5.** Know the cave conservation code and talk about measures that can be taken to promote conservation of formations and the cave environment.
- **6.** Study an aspect of speleology and discuss the findings.
