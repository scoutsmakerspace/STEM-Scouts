## Requirements

- **1.** Option 1 – Cooking for others: Plan a full, balanced menu for a group of Explorers or other people on a residential experience covering all meals for at least four days. Consider what facilities will be available.
- **2.** Option 1 – Cooking for others: Cook and serve your planned menu, demonstrating preparation and serving skills.
- **3.** Option 1 – Cooking for others: Demonstrate proper storage and cooking, including hygiene and how to prevent food poisoning.
- **4.** Option 1 – Cooking for others: Budget for your menu and assist with shopping for the ingredients.
- **5.** Option 2 – Baking: Maintain high standards of hygiene.
- **6.** Option 2 – Baking: Demonstrate how planning and preparation can make life easier when baking.
- **7.** Option 2 – Baking: Bake and cost from scratch (no packet mixes): three large cakes (20cm+) with different icings/decorations; a sweet or savoury tart (e.g., quiche or tarte tatin); 12 iced and filled cupcakes; 12 biscuits iced using the flooding method; 12 scones; a loaf of yeast bread; and 12 flatbreads.
