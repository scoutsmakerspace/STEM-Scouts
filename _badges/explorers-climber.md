## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Complete at least four different climbs, each of which should be listed  in a climbing guidebook.
- 2. 2. Know how to put on a harness and helmet, and the calls used in climbing.
- 3. 3. Understand and demonstrate the proper use of a belay device.
- 4. 4. Experience at least one session (two hours) of single-pitch climbing on a man-made climbing wall, either indoors or outdoors.
- 5. 5. Be able to use information given in a climbing guidebook. Explain the grading systems for climbs.
- 6. 7. Abseil down a face of not less than 15 metres using a safety rope.
- 7. 8. Demonstrate how to care for the equipment used and explain what you should look out for when equipment is nearing the end of its life.
