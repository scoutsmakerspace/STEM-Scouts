## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Identify and organise a fundraising initiative for either another UK registered charity of your choice or the Scout Association, to help promote and grow your local Scout group, Scouting nationally or both.
- 2. 2. Find a compelling  message – a short reason you can communicate to potential donors about your chosen cause. It must be inspiring and clear in order to raise the most money.
- 3. 3. Use social media to promote your fundraising initiative.
- 4. 4. Set a fundraising target and a date to raise it by. We suggest a minimum of £500.
- 5. 5. Call on others to help you and form a fundraising team. Motivate and inspire them to encourage others to donate.
- 6. 6. Prepare a short presentation about your fundraising challenge and submit it to the members of your local Trustee Board or your leader
