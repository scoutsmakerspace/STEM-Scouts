## Requirements

- **1.** Hold the Scout section Hill Walker Activity Badge or have completed at least five one-day journeys in the hills, each one at least 14 kilometres’ distance.
- **2.** Take a major part in planning and successfully managing at least four one-day journeys, two of which must be in terrain two areas.
- **3.** Play a major part in the planning, preparation and leadership of at least one two-day expedition, involving overnight camping in wild country
