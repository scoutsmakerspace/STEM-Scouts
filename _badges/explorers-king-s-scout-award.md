## Requirements

- **1.** Award participants must: Be aged 16 or older to start the award and complete the award before your 25th birthday, Be a member of either Explorer Scouts or Scout Network (or a combination of both) for at least 18 months to complete the award. Be a member of Explorer Scouts or Scout Network at the time you complete the award
- **2.** Complete 24 nights away as an Explorer Scout or Scout Network member, of which at least 12 must be camping.
- **3.** Complete six activities in total, two from each topic area of the ICV activities list.
- **4.** Make a presentation covering all elements of your award to a suitable audience, with the aim of inspiring and motivating others to achieve the award.
- **5.** Each  requirement  needs  to  be  signed  off  by  an  assessor.
- **1.** Hold the Gold Duke of Edinburgh's Award.
- **1.** Skill
- **2.** Physical activity
- **3.** Service
- **4.** Expedition
- **5.** Residential
