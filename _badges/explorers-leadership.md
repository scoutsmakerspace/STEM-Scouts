## Requirements

- **1.** Lead a team of Explorers by running an activity on a camp
- **2.** Lead a team of Explorers on an expedition
- **3.** Represent the views of Explorers at a forum or event
- **4.** Lead a team of explorers to deliver a project with them
- **1.** Take an active role in running or delivering an activity in a new setting.
- **1.** Reflect on the leadership behaviours and abilities you’ve displayed.
- **1.** In a setting of your choice, lead an activity or event and put your chosen actions into practice from your reflection in requirement 3.
- **2.** Visit someone in a leadership role to see what behaviours they’re displaying. Compare their actions and how they lead to the results of your own reflection in requirement 3 to support your development as a leader.
- **3.** Conduct your own research into a leader of your choice. Look at the leadership skills and behaviours they display at different times and why these might be appropriate.
