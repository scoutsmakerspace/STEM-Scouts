## Requirements

- **1.** Understand and show how to use the Scout brand, using resources available from UK HQ
- **2.** Create some media content for the Group, District or County newsletter, Scouting Magazine or online.
- **3.** Help to manage and maintain the social media pages for your local Scout Group for a period of time agreed by your Unit leader.
- **4.** Take part in or produce a package about Scouting for local TV or radio.
- **5.** Produce an audio, visual or written report about an issue affecting young people or your local Scout Group. Include different contributors such as members of the public, local personalities or public figures.
- **6.** Conduct a survey to find out young people’s views on Scouting. Share your results with the leadership team and discuss them with others in the Unit.
- **7.** Represent Explorers at a local government youth council or collective organisation youth forum.
