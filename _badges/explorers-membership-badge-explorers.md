## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Describe the various ways you can become an Explorer Scout in your area
- 2. Show you understand the Scout Promise and Law
- 3. Demonstrate an understanding of local, national and international Scouting
- 4. After discussing it with your Explorer leader, describe the Unit management and support available to you, including the Unit Constitutio
- 5. ake the Scout Promise and accept the Scout Law
