## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Understand how money works and what affects the way we use it.
- 2. 2. Build your money skills by trying activities such as trading, budgeting and finding ways to grow your money.
- 3. 3. Take action and find out how to help people with money.
- 4. 4. Take on a challenge that’ll improve your money skills and help you plan for the future.
- 5. 5. Get creative and make something that helps others learn about money in a fun way.
