## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Take part in your chosen motor sport, once you’ve agreed the activity  with your leadership team. Show how you’ve improved over a period of  time.
- 2. 2. Talk to a group of young people about the skills and techniques you have used.
- 3. 3. Explain the safety rules, and how to responsibly use the skills you have learned, to an appropriate adult.
- 4. 4. Take part in at least two competitions or demonstrations.  Talk about your performance and ways to improve with the examiner or instructor.
- 5. 5. Take up an organisational role on at least two occasions.
