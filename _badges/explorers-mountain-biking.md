## Requirements

- **1.** Regularly participate in a mountain biking discipline such as downhill, dirt jump, four cross (4X) or any other mountain bike sport as agreed by the leadership team. Show how you’ve improved over time and show evidence of this.
- **2.** Talk to a group of young people about the skills and techniques of your chosen discipline.
- **3.** Talk to an appropriate adult about the safety rules in mountain biking and how to use the skills you’ve learned responsibly.
- **4.** Take part in at least two competitions or demonstrations. Talk about your performance and ways you could improve with the examiner or instructor.
- **5.** Show that you are able to control your bike over different types of terrain.
- **6.** Show that you understand the Mountain Bike Code of Conduct.
- **7.** Be able to perform emergency repairs in the field as listed for a T1 Mountain Bike Permit.
