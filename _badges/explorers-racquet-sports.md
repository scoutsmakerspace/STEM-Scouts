## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Regularly participate in a racquet sport over a period of three months.  Demonstrate an improvement in your level of skill.
- 2. 2. Demonstrate your skills with your Unit and play your chosen sport with them.
- 3. 3. Talk about any safety procedures and rules in your chosen sport, and how to abide by them.
- 4. 4. Take part in at least two competitions or demonstrations.
