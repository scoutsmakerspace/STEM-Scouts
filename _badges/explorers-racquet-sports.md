## Requirements

- **1.** Regularly participate in a racquet sport over a period of three months.  Demonstrate an improvement in your level of skill.
- **2.** Demonstrate your skills with your Unit and play your chosen sport with them.
- **3.** Talk about any safety procedures and rules in your chosen sport, and how to abide by them.
- **4.** Take part in at least two competitions or demonstrations.
