## Requirements

- **1.** Explore first-hand accounts from people who have been involved in the space industry or witnessed its impact.
- **2.** Grow a plant without using soil.
- **3.** Find out about light pollution and how this can affect our exploration of space.
- **4.** Find out about satellites and space rovers, you could practice controlling something from a distance and learn why this is useful.
- **5.** Using a medium of your choice showcase something about space that interests you
