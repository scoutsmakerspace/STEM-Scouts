## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Regularly take part in a street sport activity such as rollerskating, skateboarding, BMX, Parkour or any other street sport you agree with your leadership team.
- 2. 2. Demonstrate skills in your chosen sport to your Unit and undertake a street sport together.
- 3. 3. Explain the safety procedures and rules in your chosen sport, and how to abide by them, including where to undertake street sports safely and responsibly.
- 4. 4. Show how to care for your equipment and explain what you should look out for when it’s nearing the end of its life.
