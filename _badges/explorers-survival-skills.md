## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Learn how to prevent and treat the effects of extreme heat (sunstroke and dehydration) and cold (hypothermia).
- **2.** 2. Demonstrate different techniques to light a fire.
- **3.** 3. Show how to build several different types of fire.
- **4.** 4. Demonstrate the correct use of international distress signals using a whistle, torch, mirror or markers.
- **5.** 5. Describe some basic actions you can take while waiting to be rescued that will keep you and your group safe, and assist your rescuers in locating you.
- **6.** 6. Learn the techniques involved in catching your food, such as fishing or snares.
- **7.** 7. Learn to identify and source local, edible plants and fruit and the risks involved in eating those incorrectly identified.
- **8.** 8. Prepare meat such as rabbit or fish to cook over an open fire and eat.
- **9.** 9. Make an item of equipment that will be of use to you on a survival exercise.
- **10.** 10. Demonstrate methods of filtering and purifying water.
- **11.** 11. Demonstrate methods of finding direction by day or night without a compass.
