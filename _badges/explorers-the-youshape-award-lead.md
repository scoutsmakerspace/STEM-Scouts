## Requirements

- Complete 1 of the following:
- 1. 1. . Help a new adult volunteer complete their induction.
- 2. 2. Lead a session for another Explorer Unit.
- 3. 3. Lead a Unit Forum for your Unit.
- 4. 4. Lead a two night camp with no adult volunteers. You’ll need a Nights Away Events Passport.
