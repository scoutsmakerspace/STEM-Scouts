## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Work with a member of your District or County/Area/Region Trustee Board to research and propose something to improve Explorer Scouts in your District or County.
- **2.** 2. Identify a group of potential adult volunteers and tell them about the benefits of volunteering with Scouts.
- **3.** 3. Take part in a project with Explorers from across your District that uses skills you’ve learned at Scouts to help people in your community.
- **4.** 4. Work with your Group Lead Volunteer, District Lead Volunteer, or County Lead Volunteer to lobby a decision maker about an issue that affects Scouts.
