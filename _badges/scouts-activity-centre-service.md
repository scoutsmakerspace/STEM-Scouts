## Requirements

- **1.** Help the warden or manager and work for at least five days at a  permanent District, County or National Scout campsite.
- **2.** Explain and show how to use three items of equipment that you've used in your work with the activity centre.
- **3.** Talk about developments and improvements you would like to see at a permanent campsite.
- **1.** Explain & demonstrate the use of computers in campsite management
- **2.** Explain & demonstrate how to take care of campers' needs while respecting wildlife
- **3.** Explain & demonstrate how to prevent frozen pipes and what to do if an overground pipe bursts or leaks
- **4.** Explain & demonstrate the need for good site drainage and clear ditches
- **5.** Explain & demonstrate rubbish disposal and how to recycle materials wherever possible
- **6.** Explain the reasons for having clean toilets
- **7.** Explain & demonstrate how to unblock a drain
- **8.** Explain & demonstrate how to use and maintain equipment for an activity you have become familiar with
- **9.** Explain other important tasks that the warden or manager has pointed out
