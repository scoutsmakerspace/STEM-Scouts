## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Research one historical aspect of flight, and share what you find out with the Troop.
- 2. 2. While you’re doing your research, visit at least one place of interest that relates to your chosen subject.
- 3. 3. Present what you have found out.
