## Requirements

- **1.** Learn to recognise 38 out of the 50 aircraft listed in The Scout Association's Aircraft Recognition List
- **2.** Describe the main features you would use to recognise six aircraft, which have been chosen by an appropriate adult.
- **3.** By yourself or with another Scout, take photographs or collect pictures of at least 10 different aircraft types.
- **4.** Visit an airfield or airport viewing area, for at least an hour.
- **5.** Recognise and name the national aircraft markings, both service and civil, of at least six countries including the United Kingdom.
- **6.** Learn the RAF and NATO system of letter designation according to aircraft function.
- **7.** Name three basic training aircraft used in private flying.
