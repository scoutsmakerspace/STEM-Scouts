## Requirements

- **1.** Learn the water safety rules and the proper precautions to take when fishing from the bank, shore line, or from a boat.
- **2.** Understand how the Countryside code should be followed by anglers, and where and how to fish legally.
- **3.** Explain how different species of fish have different habitats.
- **4.** Learn about invasive species and make sure to check, clean, and dry your equipment to stop them spreading.
- **5.** Learn the basic hygiene precautions to take when fishing.
- **6.** Go fishing at least four times in two different places.
- **7.** Choose the correct equipment and method for where you plan to fish.
- **8.** Show you can cast correctly and accurately into a target area a suitable distance away, depending on the equipment you are using.
- **9.** Show how to correctly handle a fish, unhook it and return it into the water.
