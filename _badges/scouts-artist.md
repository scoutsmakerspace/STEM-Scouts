## Requirements

- **1.** Paint, draw or illustrate a scene from a story
- **2.** Paint, draw or illustrate a person or object
- **3.** Paint, draw or illustrate a landscape
- **4.** Show a selection of your own recent work.
- **5.** Choose a favourite art form or artist to take an active interest in.
- **6.** Describe two visits you have made that are connected to your interest.
- **7.** Show that you know a lot about an aspect of your interest.
- **8.** Make a list of major events, exhibitions or venues connected with your chosen subject.
