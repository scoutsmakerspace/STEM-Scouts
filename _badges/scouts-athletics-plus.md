## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Run through an appropriate warm-up and cool-down routine using all the main muscle groups. Explain why both routines are important.
- **2.** 2. Talk about the safety rules for athletics, particularly throwing and jumping events.
- **3.** 3. Compete in any three events (two track and one field, or vice versa) and gain the points set out as indicated on the score chart below.
