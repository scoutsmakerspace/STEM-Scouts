## Requirements

- **1.** Camp as a Scout for at least 15 nights
- **2.** Lead a group of Scouts in the pitching, striking and packing of a four to six-person tent.
- **3.** Find out what to look for when choosing a campsite and in deciding the best position to pitch tents.
- **4.** Show that you understand and act on the reasons for hygiene and the importance of being safe and tidy in camp.
- **5.** Show how you store food hygienically at camp.
- **6.** Learn how to dispose of your rubbish safely and responsibly.
- **7.** Construct two useful camp gadgets.
- **8.** Plan a full menu for a weekend camp. Cook a least one full meal over an open fire.
- **9.** With others in your Troop, plan and carry out a programme of activities for a weekend camp.
