## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Take part in two circus skills
- 2. 2. Carry on putting effort into your two selected skills and show some  achievement.
- 3. 3. Demonstrate your two selected skills in front of an audience.
- 4. 4. Find out things about circus life and talk about these with an adult.
- 5. 5. Watch at least two circus or street performance events.
