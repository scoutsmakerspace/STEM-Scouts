## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Show that you know the rope types used for rock climbing.
- **2.** 2. Show that you know how to fit a climbing helmet and harness and how to tie in correctly.
- **3.** 3. Show you understand the calls used in climbing.
- **4.** 4. Show that you know how to abseil down a face. It should usually be at least 10 metres high.
- **5.** 5. Take part in four or more separate climbs.
- **6.** 6. Explain the safety rules for climbing on both natural and artificial rock faces.
- **7.** 7. Show that you’re aware of environmental  issues around climbing on natural rock faces.
- **8.** 8. Explain how to care for the equipment you used.
