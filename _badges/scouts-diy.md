## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Show you know about hazard symbols and signs (for dangers such as toxic, flammable, irritant, electrical danger or slippery surface)
- 2. Show you know about safety equipment (such as goggles, gloves, masks, ear defenders)
- 3. Show you know how and where to turn off mains supplies (such as water, electricity and gas) to the house.
- 4. Learn how to safely isolate individual electrical circuits at the consumer unit.
- 5. Show or explain how you would deal with four DIY emergencies.
- 6. Take an active part in two major DIY projects, indoors or outside.
