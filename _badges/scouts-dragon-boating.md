## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Learn the safety rules, the capsize drills, the water buddy system
- **2.** 2. Learn the procedures for loading, numbering off, stopping, bracing the boat, forward and backward paddling.
- **3.** 3. Learn the instructions and commands issued by The Dragon Boat Racing Association.
- **4.** 4. Carry out practice training sessions for a minimum of two hours.
- **5.** 5. Take part in a competitive or timed dragon boat event over a course of at least 250 metres.
