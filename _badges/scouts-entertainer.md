## Requirements

- **1.** Write and plan some original entertainment.
- **2.** Present the entertainment to an audience at a school, for your Troop or at a parents’ evening.
- **3.** Rehearse the entertainment and make sure everyone has a job to do. :
- **4.** Take an active part in a Scout show or another production.
