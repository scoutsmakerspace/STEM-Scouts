## Requirements

- **1.** Show you can catch a pony from a field or stable and tack up for riding.
- **2.** Walk, trot and canter a pony
- **3.** Jump over small fences.
- **4.** Groom your pony correctly and explain why it is necessary
- **5.** Show you know how to take care of saddlery and other equipment
- **6.** Find out about some of the basic health issues affecting horses and ponies, including worms and laminitis.
- **7.** Show you have a basic knowledge of grass management. Show you recognise poisonous plants and the results of overgrazing
- **8.** Talk about road safety for riding.
- **9.** Explain why you need a hat, body protector, stirrup and other safety equipment
