## Requirements

- **1.** Take part in either an expedition or an exploration over two days with at least three other Scouts.
- **2.** Take an active part in planning the expedition or exploration
- **3.** During the expedition or exploration: Play a full part in the team, Use a map or other navigation device to keep track of where you are, Cook and eat at least one hot meal, Do a task, investigation, or exploration as agreed with your Leader.
- **4.** Produce an individual report or presentation within the three weeks following your expedition or exploration.
