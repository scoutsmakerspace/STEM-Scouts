## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Explain what action should be taken and why, if you discover a fire has broken out in the home or at camp.
- 2. 2. Explain the process of combustion.
- 3. 3. Be aware of dangers in the home and understand the fire precautions necessary.
- 4. 4. Explain the benefits of installing smoke detectors.
- 5. 5. Learn what the dangers of campfires are and what precautions should be taken.
- 6. 6. Find out about the causes of heath and grass fires.
- 7. 7. Learn how to make an emergency call for the fire service.
- 8. 8. Identify different fire extinguisher types such as water, dry powder, foam and carbon dioxide.
- 9. 9. Find out what to do if a person’s clothes are on fire.
- 10. 10. Talk to your family about what to do in the event of a fire at home.
