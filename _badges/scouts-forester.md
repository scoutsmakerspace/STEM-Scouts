## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Identify at least eight common types of tree that grow in your area, including both deciduous and coniferous.
- **2.** 2. Find out how to identify trees using identification keys.
- **3.** 3. Prepare the soil and successfully transplant a young tree.
- **4.** 4. Find out how both natural woodland and commercial forests are managed.
- **5.** 5. Show how to select, use and care for forestry equipment, and know the safety issues involved.
- **6.** 6. Show how to fell and trim out a tree or lay a hedge.
