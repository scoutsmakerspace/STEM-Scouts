## Requirements

- **1.** Show that you know about the Global Positioning System (GPS).
- **2.** Programme a handheld GPS.
- **3.** Show that you know the difference between Ordnance Survey, and latitude and longitude coordinates.
- **4.** Using an Ordnance Survey map (1:25000 or 1:50000 scale) to plan a route of at least 4km that contains a minimum of 10 waypoints.
- **5.** Sign up to a geocaching website.
- **6.** Show that you understand the safety and environmental aspects of geocaching, such as the Highway Code, Countryside Code and the Geocaching Association of Great Britain (GAGB) guidelines.
- **7.** Find five geocaches using a GPS. At least three geocaches must be ‘multi-caches’, with at least two waypoints.
- **8.** With the help of an adult create two caches.
