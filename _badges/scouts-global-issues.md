## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Identify where your clothing or other belongings have been made.
- **2.** 2. Investigate a recent natural disaster by looking at photos, news articles or videos online.
- **3.** 3. Find out about an international sporting event and discuss with your Troop how sporting events can contribute to international peace and understanding.
- **4.** 4. In small groups, learn about an international health issue and use a creative way to share what you have learnt with your Troop.
- **5.** 5. Think about the rights that we have in the UK (like the right to vote, the right to go to school and equal gender rights).
