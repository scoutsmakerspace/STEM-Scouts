## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Find out about the World Scout Movement’s history and what it does today.
- **2.** 2. Take part in a traditional craft or creative activity from another country.
- **3.** 3. In a language other than your own, introduce yourself and say a few basic, useful, everyday phrases.
- **4.** 4. Take part in one of an event individually or with the Troop and report back to other Scout.
