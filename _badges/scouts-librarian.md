## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Show that you know how to look after books and e-readers.
- **2.** 2. Show that you can use a library catalogue.
- **3.** 3. Explain how fiction and non-fiction books are arranged on the shelves.
- **4.** 4. Learn what is meant by a reference book or material.
- **5.** 5. Show how you would search for information using the internet.
- **6.** 6. Talk to your assessor about books you have read and why you enjoyed theminformation you found from books or websitesgetting books on the internet for e-readers.
