## Requirements

- **1.** Show that you know the local area surrounding your home or Scout Headquarters, up to a radius of 2 kilometres in suburban districts and 5 kilometres in rural districts.
- **2.** Use a street map to point out six locations from step 1. From your home or Scout Headquarters, show the quickest route to one of the places.
- **1.** Gain a general knowledge of what parts of the country are served from your local airport, mainline railway and coach stations.
- **2.** Find out how to reach the local airport, mainline railway and coach stations, and major tourist attractions from your Scout Headquarters or home.
- **3.** Show how to use a map of your district.
- **4.** Use it to point out any six places of interest. Show how to get to these places from your Scout Headquarters or home.
- **5.** Give clear directions to a place of interest 8 kilometres away, to a person travelling by car or public transport.
- **6.** Find out which major local roads link to the motorway and A-road network and the main cities these roads serve.
- **1.** Study an aspect of national history, local history or family heritage.
- **2.** Over a period agreed with your leader, get involved in a project to help preserve some aspect of national or local heritage.
