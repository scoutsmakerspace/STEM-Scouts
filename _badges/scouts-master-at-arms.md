## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Attend regular training sessions in a relevant activity like fencing, shooting or archery.
- 2. 2. Learn the safety rules associated with your activity and show how to follow them.
- 3. 3. Take part in your chosen activity at an officially supervised contest.
