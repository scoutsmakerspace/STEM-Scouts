## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Produce and give a presentation about Scouting.
- **2.** 2. Write a press release about a Scout event that has taken place.
- **3.** 3. Find out about local media outlets such as radio, TV, newspapers and online.
- **4.** 4. Prepare and present an audio or video package about a Scouting event or activity.
- **5.** 5. Prepare a creative display about your Troop or Group that can be exhibited in your local library, information centre or public place.
- **6.** 6. Interview a local public figure or someone in the local news, such as a church leader, politician or celebrity and present the interview to your Troop.
- **7.** 7. Produce some media which can be used within Scouting, such as a district newsletter story or a piece of website content.
