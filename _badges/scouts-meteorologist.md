## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Explain how each of these are measured:Wind force and direction, Cloud type and extent, Temperature, Pressure, Rainfall, Humidity
- 2. 2. Record the weather conditions every day for two weeks, or once a week for three months.
- 3. 3. Identify different cloud types.
- 4. 4. What do ‘warm’ and ‘cold’ air masses in summer and winter do to the typical weather in your area?
- 5. 5. Explain how weather forecasts are created.
- 6. 6. Show that you understand a synoptic weather map, including fronts and isobars.
