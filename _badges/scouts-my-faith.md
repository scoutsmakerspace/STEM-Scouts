## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Take an active part in your place of worship.
- 2. 2. Find out more about the origins of your faith.
- 3. 3. Explore something about the history of your faith at a local, national or international level.
- 4. 4. Explain to an adult some of the teachings of your faith.
