## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Learn about the map colours and common symbols used on an orienteering map.
- **2.** 2. Orientate a map using either terrain or a compass.
- **3.** 3. Complete three courses at orienteering events recognised by British Orienteering or another similar standard.
- **4.** 4. Show you know the safety procedures, basic first aid, appropriate clothing and equipment for countryside navigation.
- **5.** 5. Show that you know the Countryside Code.
