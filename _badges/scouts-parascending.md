## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Take part in a parascending course.
- 2. Learn the main characteristics and different types of parachutes used by  parascenders.
- 3. 3. Show a basic knowledge of the theory of flight.
- 4. 4. Experience at least four parascending flights.
