## Requirements

- **1.** 12 photos or 6 B&W photos
- **2.** Show that you know the main settings on a digital camera or a smartphone camera.
- **3.** Describe what accessories are available to use with digital cameras or smartphone cameras.
- **4.** Edit a selection of your images, using editing software on a computer or using an app on a smartphone.
- **5.** Diagnose typical faults that happen at the photographing or editing stages, such as over or under exposure and high or low contrast.
- **6.** Show that you know how to care for a digital camera or smartphone camera.
