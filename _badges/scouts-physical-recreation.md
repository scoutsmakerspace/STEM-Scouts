## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Regularly take part in an active sport or physical pursuit, which you haven’t already gained an activity badge for.
- **2.** 2. Show a reasonable level of skill in your sport or pursuit.
- **3.** 3. Explain the rules or guidelines that govern the sport or pursuit you chose.
- **4.** 4. Show how you would prepare before taking part in your sport or pursuit.
- **5.** 5. Explain how to care for the equipment you use.
