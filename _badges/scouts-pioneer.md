## Requirements

- **1.** As a member of a group, take part in an indoor pioneering project, like constructing a catapult or chariot
- **2.** As a member of a group, take part in an outdoor pioneering project, like building a monkey bridge, a raft or parallel runway
- **3.** Show the correct way to do a whipping or safe rope sealing
- **4.** Show the correct way to splice a rope
- **5.** Show the correct way to coil and store a rope
- **6.** Show the correct way to use levers to extract objects or move heavy weights
- **7.** Show the correct way to be safe in pioneering projects. Explain why it's important
- **8.** Name and tie at least six knots and three lashings that are useful in pioneering
