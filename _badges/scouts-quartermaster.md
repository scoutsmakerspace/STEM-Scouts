## Requirements

- **1.** Show ability in the listed areas
- **2.** Keep a simple record showing equipment issued and returned.
- **3.** Show you understand that general tidiness is the secret of good quartermastering.
- **1.** During the experience show you’re capable in at least three areas
- **2.** Keep simple records, showing the equipment issued and returned.
- **3.** Keep a portable first aid kit well stocked and maintained.
- **4.** Choose tools to take to camp, to complete emergency repairs on equipment.
- **5.** Show you understand that general tidiness is the secret of good quartermastering.
