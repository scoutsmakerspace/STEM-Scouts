## Requirements

- **1.** Regularly take part in physical activities over a period of four to six weeks.
- **2.** Take part in at least three activities that require a number of problem-solving skills.
- **3.** Show you understand why eating a sensible diet and getting enough sleep is important
- **4.** Do some research so that you can explain the dangers and harmful effects of smoking, alcohol and drugs
- **5.** Mend or customise an item of clothing
- **6.** Cook and serve a two-course meal, for at least four people
- **7.** Fix a puncture or a dropped chain on a bike
- **8.** Wash up after a meal, making sure everything is clean and dry
- **9.** Use a washing machine to wash a load of clothes
- **10.** Iron your uniform shirt
- **11.** Change a lightbulb, in a ceiling light
- **12.** Set a heating timer and thermostat as needed for the time of year
- **13.** Clean a toilet, hob or oven
- **14.** Do another similar home skill
