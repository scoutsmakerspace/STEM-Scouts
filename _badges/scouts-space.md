## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Find out what happens to an astronaut's body in space. Learn how the lack of gravity affects their body, and what they do to stay fit.
- 2. 2. Learn about the speed of light and how we use light years to measure distances in space.
- 3. 3. Discover how different space technologies have helped us to understand and explore the universe.
- 4. 4. Find out what a black hole is, how it forms and how we can detect where they are in space.
- 5. 5. Discover something new about the universe and share it with the group.
- 6. 6. You can use the stars to find your way. Explore how you can navigate using stars and constellations.
- 7. 7. Imagine you’re planning an expedition to a distant solar system. Design how you’ll travel there and think about what you will need to take with you.
