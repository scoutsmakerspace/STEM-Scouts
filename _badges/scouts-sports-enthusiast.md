## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Explain the rules governing your favourite sport.
- **2.** 2. Describe the levels of achievement within your chosen sport locally, nationally or internationally.
- **3.** 3. Show that you know some of the personalities, champions or other experts in your chosen sport.
- **4.** 4. Talk about the equipment needed for the sport.
- **5.** 5. Describe a recent major event, championship or landmark in the sport.
- **6.** 6. Explain how you follow your sport. How do you keep up to date with developments?
