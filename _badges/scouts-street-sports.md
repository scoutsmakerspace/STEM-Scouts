## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Take part in a street sport like skateboarding, roller or in-line skating, Parkour or another street sport agreed by your leadership team.
- **2.** 2. Own or use equipment for a street sport.
- **3.** 3. Explain how to care for the equipment used and explain what you should look out for when equipment is nearing the end of its life.
- **4.** 4. Show that you’re skilled in your chosen street sport and show how you’ve improved over three months.
- **5.** 5. Explain the safety rules for your chosen sport including where to undertake street sports safely and responsibly.
