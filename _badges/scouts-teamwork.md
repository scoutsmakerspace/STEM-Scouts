## Requirements

- **1.** On at least three separate occasions, be part of a Scout team, where you work together to achieve a goal.
- **2.** Give at least three examples of when you’ve been in different types of teams.
- **3.** Take part in at least three team building activities that you have not tried before.
- **4.** Take an active part in at least four Troop or Patrol Forums.
