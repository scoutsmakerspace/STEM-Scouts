## Requirements

- **1.** 1. Identify and organise a fundraising initiative.
- **2.** 2. Think of a short reason that tells potential donors about your chosen cause.
- **3.** 3. Set a target and a date by which you’ll have raised the money.
- **4.** 4. Write a short report about your fundraising challenge and submit it to your leader, Troop Forum or Patrol Leaders’ Council.
