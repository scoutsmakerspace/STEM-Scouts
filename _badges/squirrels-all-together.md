## Requirements

- **1.** Games are much more fun with friends. Play some team games with your friends at Squirrels and work together to have fun.
- **2.** You can do almost anything with help from good friends. Work with others to be a great team member – remember to take turns, share things, help each other, and follow instructions.
- **3.** The things we decide to do affect other people too. Make a choice for yourself and your team – remember to think about what other people might want and how they might feel.
- **4.** Everyone needs a little help sometimes. Help your leader to show someone new what Squirrel Scouts is all about.
