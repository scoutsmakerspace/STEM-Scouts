## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. There are lots of different ways to be active. Explore the different ways that you can move your body and see how they feel.
- 2. Playing games is lots of fun. Learn to play a new game using different equipment.
- 3. Games are even more fun when you play with your friends. Do your best to help your friends play a game.
