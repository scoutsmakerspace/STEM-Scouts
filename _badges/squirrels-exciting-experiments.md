## Requirements

- **1.** We find out about the world around us using our senses. Try some activities to explore all of your different senses. What can you see, hear, smell, taste, and feel?
- **2.** Scientists think about how things might work and do experiments to find out if they’re right. Get stuck in and try some experiments together. What do you think will happen?
- **3.** Being a scientist is all about sharing what you’ve learned. Tell a friend about your experiments and what you found out.
