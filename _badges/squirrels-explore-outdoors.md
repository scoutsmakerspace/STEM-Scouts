## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. Every great adventure starts with a great plan. Work together to think about some different places you could visit, what you should wear, and what you’ll need to take with you.
- 2. It’s time to get outside! Explore some different environments, use a compass to see which direction you’re going, and keep track of all the interesting things you see and hear.
- 3. The fun doesn’t have to stop when you get home. Tell a friend about your adventure and think about how you could make your next trip even better.
