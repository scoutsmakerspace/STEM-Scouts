## Requirements

- **1.** There are lots of local superheroes doing great things near where you live. Find out who they are and what they do to help.
- **2.** Anyone can be a superhero if they use their skills to make the world a better place. Design your superhero costume, choose your powers, and think about how you’ll use your powers to help other people.
- **3.** Now it’s time to put your superpowers to good use. Work with your group to make a difference in your local area.
