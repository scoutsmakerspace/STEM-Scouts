## Requirements

- Complete ALL of the following:
- 1. 1. Go to Squirrels, get to know the leaders and the other young people, and find out what you’ll get up to.
- 2. 2. Discover the different badges you can earn and find out what happens at your Investiture (a special celebration that happens when you make your Promise).
- 3. 3. Learn your Promise and when you’re ready, get invested.
- 4. 4. Find out about the history of Scouts and the worldwide Scout family.
