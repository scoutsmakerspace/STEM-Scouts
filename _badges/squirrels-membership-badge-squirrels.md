## Requirements

- **1.** Go to Squirrels, get to know the leaders and the other young people, and find out what you’ll get up to.
- **2.** Discover the different badges you can earn and find out what happens at your Investiture (a special celebration that happens when you make your Promise).
- **3.** Learn your Promise and when you’re ready, get invested.
- **4.** Find out about the history of Scouts and the worldwide Scout family.
