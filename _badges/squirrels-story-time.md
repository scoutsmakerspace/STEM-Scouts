## Requirements

- **1.** When we hear stories, we can imagine going to different places and getting involved in lots of adventures. Choose a story to listen to with your friends and talk about it afterwards.
- **2.** Stories can be about almost anything: people, animals, or magical creatures. What sort of stories do you like the most? Tell someone about your favourite story.
- **3.** Anyone can be a storyteller. Now it’s your turn to be in charge! Make up your own story and tell someone about it.
