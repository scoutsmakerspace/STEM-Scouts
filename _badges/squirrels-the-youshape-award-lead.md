## Requirements

- Complete 1 of the following:
- 1. Help with an activity during a meeting, supported by a Young Leader or adult volunteer.
- 2. Lead part of a story.
- 3. Lead a game.
- 4. Use materials to explore your Squirrels YouShape Award themes further.
- 5. Welcome a new Squirrel into your Drey.
