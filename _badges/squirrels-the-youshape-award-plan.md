## Requirements

- **1.** Think of an idea for a group activity and tell a Squirrel volunteer about it.
- **2.** Share an idea during a group chat
- **3.** Choose something to explore in a Squirrel meeting with your volunteer
- **4.** As a Drey, plan a game to play.
- **5.** Suggest somewhere that your Drey could visit in your local area.
