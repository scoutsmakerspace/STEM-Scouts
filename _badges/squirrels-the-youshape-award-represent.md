## Requirements

- **1.** Celebrate the Squirrels YouShape Award by making a song, show or work of art based on what you’ve experienced. Share it with your family, community or Scout group.
- **2.** Create a painting, storybook or a poster based on what you’ve experienced during the Squirrels YouShape Award.
- **3.** You could share any of the above with your local youth lead or someone from your local area’s wider volunteer team.
- **4.** Tell an adult volunteer about something you’ve learned during the Squirrels YouShape Award that you could use to help people in your community.
- **5.** Get in touch with a local decision maker, tell them what you like about Squirrels, and ask them to help Scouts in your area.
