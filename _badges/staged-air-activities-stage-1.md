## Requirements

- **1.** Make an aircraft out of paper and see how well it flies.
- **2.** Identify four different types of aircraft.
- **3.** Describe any aircraft (including fictional) you’d like to fly in and explain why.
- **4.** Talk to someone who's flown in an aircraft.
- **5.** Learn about the different jobs an aircraft can do.
