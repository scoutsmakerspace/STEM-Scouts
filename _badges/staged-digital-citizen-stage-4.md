## Requirements

- **1.** Show that you understand your rights regarding online information
- **2.** Take part in a debate on a topic that you think there may be lots of misinformation about online.
- **3.** Create a digital version of the Scout Law which shows how each law can be followed when online.
- **4.** Complete an online training course of your choice and share some of what you learned with other young people.
- **5.** In a team that includes young people from other parts of the country or world, use a digital collaboration tool of your choice to achieve: Something that benefits Scouts, something that benefits a charity or cause you all care about, or something that promotes young people’s voices within national decision makers.
