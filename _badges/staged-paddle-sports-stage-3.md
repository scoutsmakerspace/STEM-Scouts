## Requirements

- **1.** With help, show more than one safe method for lifting and carrying your vessel.
- **2.** Demonstrate two different ways of safely launching your vessel.
- **3.** Show you can get in and out/on or off of your vessel without help.
- **4.** Show you can steer around a figure of eight course.
- **5.** Paddle forwards and backwards in a straight line, keeping good posture.
- **6.** Show you can stop the vessel safely when it’s moving back and forth.
- **7.** Capsize, swim to the shore and empty the boat of water if necessary.
- **8.** Assist someone else back into their vessel following a capsize.
- **9.** Gain the British Canoeing Paddle Explore Award.
