## Requirements

- **1.** Launch and recover your boat in any wind direction.
- **2.** Set up your boat according to weather conditions, using sail and rig controls.
- **3.** Show that you’re able to recover a man overboard.
- **4.** Show that you know about: International Regulations for Preventing Collision at Sea (IRPCS). Beaufort Scale, Synoptic charts, Tidal ebb and flow, Spring and neap tides
- **5.** Complete the RYA Youth Sailing Scheme Stage 4.
