## Requirements

- **1.** Identify different types of snowsports.
- **2.** Name three places you could safely take part in snowsport activities.
- **3.** Take part in a warm-up activity to prepare you for a snowsport activity such as skiing or snowboarding.
- **4.** Dress properly for your chosen activity.
- **5.** Take part in a taster session that covers: Naming equipment used, How to get in and out of your skis or snowboard, Balancing on your skis or snowboard, Moving around on your skis or snowboard
