## Requirements

- **1.** Show you understand what equipment to carry for the different two types of activity
- **2.** Show how to look after yourself and others in the event of not being able to return down the mountain, or being stranded on a route.
- **3.** Show your ability to use a ski map or resort map and identify where you are on the map.
- **4.** Plan a half-day's activity
- **5.** Participate in a snowsport activity different from that which the other requirements have been completed in.
