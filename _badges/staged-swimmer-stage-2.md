## Requirements

- **1.** Learn about where to swim safely in different locations and find out some safety rules for a swimming pool.
- **2.** Take part in a warm up before getting in the pool.
- **3.** Demonstrate how to get in and out of the water safely without support.
- **4.** Swim 10 metres on your front, with or without support.
- **5.** Swim 10 metres on your back, with or without support.
- **6.** Perform a log roll of at least 180 degrees in the water, either on your front or on your back.
- **7.** Demonstrate a push and glide into the middle of the pool on both your front and your back.
- **8.** Starting from the side of the pool, retrieve a floating object with your face submerged in the water.
- **9.** Float on your front and then your back, holding each float for at least 5 seconds.
