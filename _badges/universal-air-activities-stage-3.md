## Requirements

- **1.** Describe how a wing generates lift and the conditions that can cause an aircraft to stall.
- **2.** Name the main control surfaces of an aeroplane and how they work.
- **3.** Flight Time Calculation Test
- **1.** Draw a diagram or make a model of an airfield and name the key features.
- **2.** Find out about unmanned aircraft, such as drones.
- **3.** Try out a flight simulator. This could be done online, on an app, on a computer game or as an immersive experience.
- **4.** Look at an online weather forecast and understand how different conditions (such as wind speed, visibility and cloud cover) impact flying.
- **5.** Know the role of an airfield marshal.
- **6.** Explore a place of aviation interest, such as an airfield, air display, aviation museum or through a virtual tour.
- **7.** Send and receive a message using the phonetic alphabet, then explain why it's used to communicate in aviation.
