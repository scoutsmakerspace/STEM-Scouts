## Requirements

- **1.** In a creative way of your choice, map out your regular digital actions, such as searches, clicks or posts.
- **2.** Learn how to create a secure password and make one of your own.
- **3.** Share three ways young people can respond positively when someone’s being unkind or dishonest, including telling an adult if someone’s making you feel uncomfortable.
- **4.** Use an online service to learn a new skill and show others what you’ve learnt.
