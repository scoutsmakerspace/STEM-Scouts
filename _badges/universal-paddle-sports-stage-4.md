## Requirements

- Choose 1 of the following options:
- Option 1:
- 1. Choose two of the disciplines and paddle your vessel for at least 200m in each: Crew: Choose from K2 (two-man kayak), K4 (four-man kayak), C2 (two-man canoe), or OC2 (two-man canoe with outrigger), Flat water, White water, Touring, Short boat, Ergo
- 2. Take part in at least two of the following activities that you have not tried before: Freestyle, Marathon, Polo, Slalom, Sprint, Surf, Time trial, Wild water Option 2:
- 1. Gain two of the following British Canoeing Personal Performance Awards: Surf Kayak Award, Sea Kayak Award, Canoe Award, Flat Water Freestyle Award ,Polo Explore Award, Racing Explore Award, Flat Water Raft Racing Award ,Slalom Explore Award, SUP Sheltered Water Award, Touring Award, White Water Award ,Wild Water Racing Explore Award
