## Requirements

- Choose 1 of the following options:
- Option 1:
- 1. Prepare, launch and recover your boat in an onshore wind.
- 2. Show the correct way to store your boat ashore.
- 3. Show you can right a capsized dinghy as helm or crew.
- 4. Show you know how to recover a man overboard.
- 5. Demonstrate ability in the following: Sail setting, Balance, Trim, Course made good, Centreboard, Taking upwind, Gybing from a training run, Coming alongside a moored boat, Picking up a mooring
- 6. Learn how a sail works and its basic aerodynamics.
- 7. When sailing, show you can understand and use basic terminology, such as windward, leeward, and bear away. Option 2:
- 1. Gain a Personal Activity Permit for Dinghy Sailing. Option 3:
- 1. Complete the RYA Youth Sailing Scheme Stage 3.
