## Requirements

- Choose 1 of the following options:
- Option 1:
- 1. Launch and recover your boat in any wind direction.
- 2. Set up your boat according to weather conditions, using sail and rig controls.
- 3. Show that you’re able to recover a man overboard.
- 4. Show that you know about: International Regulations for Preventing Collision at Sea (IRPCS). Beaufort Scale, Synoptic charts, Tidal ebb and flow, Spring and neap tides Option 2:
- 1. Complete the RYA Youth Sailing Scheme Stage 4.
