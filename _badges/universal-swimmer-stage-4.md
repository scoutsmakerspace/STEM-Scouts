## Requirements

- **1.** Learn about where to swim safely in different locations and find out about rescue equipment.
- **2.** Take part in a warm up before getting in the pool.
- **3.** Demonstrate how to get in and out of the water safely using two different methods of entry.
- **4.** Swim confidently on your front for 25 metres without stopping.
- **5.** Swim confidently on your back for 25 metres without stopping.
- **6.** Swim 25 metres using either the breaststroke or butterfly swimming techniques.
- **7.** Demonstrate the breaststroke leg movements whilst holding onto the side of the pool.
- **8.** Swim 100 metres in a stroke of your choice.
- **9.** Tread water for 30 seconds.
- **10.** Swim in clothes for 10 metres.
- **11.** Scull for 5 metres on your front or your back.
