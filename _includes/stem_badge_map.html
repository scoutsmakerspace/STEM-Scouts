How this mapping works

This is a starter map to help leaders plan STEM sessions. It highlights requirements whose wording strongly suggests STEM skills (coding, circuits, scientific testing, design/engineering, radio/space, maths/data). It’s not a rulebook — leaders still decide what genuinely fits their programme.
